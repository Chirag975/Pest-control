Mosquito Control by ABC Pest Control Services



Mosquitoes Control by ABC Pest Control Services

Fogging is a method of controlling outdoor mosquito, flies and other such pests. The chemical fogging solution is heated to produce thick fog or smoke to kill mosquitos and other pests. It is temporary solution and does not prevent mosquitos from re-entering the area. It only reduces the mosquito population for a few days.

Important guidelines for fogging

  • After fogging is done in the affected area stay out of the treated area for an hour to minimize breathing in spray mist.
  • Close all doors and windows and switch of your air conditioner units for an hour after fogging is complete.
  • Bring your laundry and other usable items inside before fogging begins.
  • If you have pets in home, cover pet food and water dishes before fogging begins.
  • If fogging just occurred, minimize your contact with surfaces exposed to fogging chemical.
  • Cover your food items before fogging begins. Rinse all homegrown fruits and vegetables thoroughly with water before cooking or eating.

What is included in fogging service?

For fogging, ABC pest control mix fogging chemical (mostly pyrethrin) with water or oil and apply it as an ultra-low-volume spray that kills flying adult mosquitoes and flies. ULV sprayers release very tiny aerosol droplets that stay in the air and kill adult mosquitoes on contact. The effect of fogging does not last very long.
