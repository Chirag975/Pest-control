Bed Bugs Control by India Pest Control Services



Bed bugs are becoming more and more active across the globe. Over the last 5 years we have seen a significant rise in the number of recorded bed bug incidents in all parts of the world.

Danger Signs

  • Live insects
  • Small blood spots on bed sheets
  • Faecal pellets
  • Cast nymphal skins
  • Distinctive sweet & sickly smell.

Potential Harm

  • Customers can be bitten
  • Bed bugs move around easily but are difficult to treat
  • Any bed bug infestation can quickly reach an epidemic level if not treated in a professional way.

Business Consequences

Bed bugs use numerous entry points for invading your premises – so your business is constantly exposed to the risks associated with infestation. There is very little that can be done to prevent them from entering any premises.

Consequences for Businesses

  • Customer complaints
  • Damage to brand and reputation
  • Loss of profit
  • Possible legal proceedings
  • High cost of treatment

Consequences for Individuals

  • Allergic reactions
  • Discomfort
  • Inconvenience
  • Risk of taking them home
  • Cost of pest control treatment if taken home

Immediate Steps to Take

  • Let us help you manage the risk of future infestations through Risk assessment, Training and Proactive Inspections.
  • Fill in our DIY risk assessment questionnaire to get an indication of the risk of suffering an infestation in your premises.
  • Bed bugs are very difficult to find and treat and for this reason we recommend that you do not try to get rid of an infestation without professionals.
