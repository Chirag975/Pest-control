Your protection is our priority.



As an essential services provider, ABC Pest Control is committed to protecting your home and providing peace of mind. ABC Pest Control has always been a trusted partner for pest prevention. Not all household cleaning items are effective at eliminating harmful viruses and bacteria. Even if they are effective, they must be used frequently, in the right manner and on all appropriate surfaces and items.

How our service works:

  • Because safety is our top priority, all technicians who enter your home will be wearing personal protective equipment, including respiratory protection, protective suits, gloves and goggles.

  • ABC Pest Control has enhanced safety and cleanliness protocols, including regular cleaning and disinfecting of company vehicles and service equipment to avoid cross-contamination.
